About Me

I am a Large Language Model Engineer at Baichuan Inc., where I focus on advancing the capabilities of foundation models through post-training optimization and enhancing their reasoning abilities. My work sits at the intersection of cutting-edge AI research and practical engineering, aiming to bridge the gap between theoretical innovation and real-world applications.

Before joining Baichuan Inc., I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Huajun Chen and Prof. Wen Zhang. During my doctoral studies, I was a visiting researcher at The University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Prof. Jeff Z. Pan.
